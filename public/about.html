About

Hi, I'm Livin.

I'm a Solutions Architect based in Cochin, India. Eleven years in, I've moved between hands-on engineering and architecture leadership — bootstrapping a startup to acqui-hire, scaling consumer Android apps to 10M+ downloads, building HIPAA / HITRUST-compliant clinical AI pipelines, and most recently shipping production GenAI platforms — multi-agent RAG, semantic search, and evaluation frameworks — on AWS.

I gravitate toward problems that sit at the seam between product and infrastructure: multi-agent orchestration, retrieval and ranking, evaluation frameworks, and the boring-but-load-bearing glue that decides whether a system feels fast, accurate, and audit-clean under real enterprise scrutiny.

Currently leading AI engineering at Centric Software via Galent — building a multi-agent GenAI PLM suite deployed to Tier-1 fashion brands including Asics and Under Armour. Open to staff / principal engineering and architecture conversations.
